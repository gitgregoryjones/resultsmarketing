Elsie - Founder

Elsie is one of the most trusted and respected leaders in the multicultural beauty industry, having influenced the sales destiny of national and international hair care brands representing in the hundreds of millions in global sales.

With over 25 years of experience, she founded Results Marketing to bridge the gap between brands and multicultural consumers. Her expertise spans retail strategy, market expansion, and brand positioning across North America.

Prior to founding Results Marketing, Elsie served as VP of Sales for several Fortune 500 beauty companies, where she increased market share by an average of 35% across multicultural segments.

Elsie holds an MBA from Stanford University and serves on the board of the National Beauty Association. She is a frequent speaker at industry conferences and has been featured in Beauty Inc and WWD.

Elsie Cardell - President & Owner

Elsie Cardell is one of the most trusted and respected leaders in the beauty industry, having shaped the commercial success of national and international hair care brands representing hundreds of millions in global sales. With more than 30 years of experience growing brands at retail, she brings a rare combination of strategic clarity, deep market insight, and unwavering commitment to every partnership. As President and Owner of Results Sales & Marketing LLC, Elsie leads a boutique sales management firm known for delivering sustainable growth and expanding distribution for small to midsize manufacturers across mass, specialty, and salon retail. Her leadership is grounded in the belief that client goals should be carried with the same care, integrity, and determination as her own. Her career includes founding Signature Products Inc., acquiring and growing the AROSCI brand internationally, and serving as Vice President within L’Oréal USA overseeing U.S., Canada, and Caribbean commercial operations. Affectionately known as “AuntiE™,” Elsie is also a mentor, speaker, and motivator whose “Believe and Receive” inspiration segment airs internationally. Her accolades include features and awards from Who’s Who of Chicago, Salon Sense, DHC Beauty Vanguard, Ladies of Virtue, and Rolling Out Magazine.

April Singleton - VP
April Singleton is a forward-thinking operations and business solutions executive with more than 20 years of experience helping organizations

April Singleton is a forward-thinking operations and business solutions executive with more than 20 years of experience helping organizations develop and execute strategies that drive growth, efficiency, and organizational transformation. Her expertise spans operational turnarounds, program development, corporate solutions, recruitment, logistics, promotions, and budgeting. April holds an MBA in Human Resources from Roosevelt University and a BBA in Accounting and International Business, along with a professional real estate license. For more than a decade, she owned and operated a professional services firm specializing in management, operations, and accounting, delivering scalable solutions that support organizational agility and growth. At Results, April provides superior organizational leadership as VP–Operations, supporting manufacturers across domestic and international distribution networks. She manages national teams of up to 100 people, ensuring precision, excellence, and an unmatched standard of client care. Her belief that “the most successful people are those who have mastered the art of change” guides her leadership philosophy. April is also deeply committed to service, actively supporting Catholic Charities of the Archdiocese of Chicago. She was honored with the 2020 Compassion in Action Award for her contributions to families, seniors, and individuals in need.

Natarsha J. Wilson - Senior Business Development Manager

Natarsha Wilson began her HBA career in the 1980s as a Sales Manager with Theons Nail Polish, building accounts through product education and sales-driving initiatives. She later joined Soft Sheen Products in its inaugural Professional Merchandisers Program, marking the beginning of a distinguished 30-year career across Fortune 500 companies and leading independent brands. Her expertise spans brand development, sales, merchandising, retail strategy, and relationship management. Natarsha has consistently delivered an average of 30% sales increases over the last 25 years, grounded in her understanding of how to merchandise products for maximum retail impact. A skilled relationship builder on every level, Natarsha brings insight, discipline, and strategic clarity to every assignment. Her commitment extends beyond the industry, serving in various breast cancer initiatives, Hosea Feed the Homeless, community leadership in Sylvan Hills, and ministries at Word of Faith Family Worship Center.
