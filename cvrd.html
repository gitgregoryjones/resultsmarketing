Being a Contractor Is Hard...

The Problem

With Fringe Benefit Compliance

Government contractors face a unique set of challenges with health and welfare benefits management. You're caught between fixed H&W rates and unpredictable hours worked, coupled with increased premium rates, leaving you with shrinking margins and frustrated employees. We understand the pressure you're under to solve both problems simultaneously.

The Solution

For Cost Containment & Engagement

Our dual approach merges ICHRA implementation with deep GovCon expertise to maximize your fringe dollar impact. We convert rigid benefit structures into flexible contributions that adapt to your variable workforce while maintaining alignment with federal requirements. Our SaaS and services solution handles fringe tracking and transparency through our end-to-end fringe benefit administration platform.

Forecast for the Future & Empower Employee Choice

*ICHRA (Individual Coverage Health Reimbursement Account) is a benefits model, ERISA-covered plan that allows the employer to define a fixed allowance, and the employee chooses any qualified individual plan that meets their needs.
