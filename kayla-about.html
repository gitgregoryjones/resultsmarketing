About Me

Since I was 3 years old, I have always liked movies and cartoons with Finding Nemo igniting my passion. My artwork has reflected my personal life such as the importance of my family, grieving the loss of loved ones, and learning to love who I truly am. I am attending SCAD in pursuit of a 2D animation degree focusing on storyboarding. Through animation, I can express who I am, both the good and bad parts of my life or something made that I can personally relate to that I feel the audience can also relate to.
