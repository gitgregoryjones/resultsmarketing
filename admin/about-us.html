Elsie - Founder
Founder with 25+ years revolutionizing multicultural beauty retail. Pioneer in brand strategy and market expansion across North America.

Elsie is one of the most trusted and respected leaders in the multicultural beauty industry, having influenced the sales destiny of national and international hair care brands representing in the hundreds of millions in global sales.

With over 25 years of experience, she founded Results Marketing to bridge the gap between brands and multicultural consumers. Her expertise spans retail strategy, market expansion, and brand positioning across North America.

Prior to founding Results Marketing, Elsie served as VP of Sales for several Fortune 500 beauty companies, where she increased market share by an average of 35% across multicultural segments.

Elsie holds an MBA from Stanford University and serves on the board of the National Beauty Association. She is a frequent speaker at industry conferences and has been featured in Beauty Inc and WWD.

Angie - VP
VP specializing in sales strategy and retail execution with 15+ years driving growth for beauty brands.

Angie is one of the most trusted and respected leaders in the multicultural beauty industry, having influenced the sales destiny of national and international hair care brands representing in the hundreds of millions in global sales.

She brings 15 years of expertise in sales strategy, retail execution, and team leadership. Angie has successfully launched over 50 products into multicultural markets and developed training programs for major retailers including Walmart, Target, and Ulta Beauty.

Her data-driven approach to market analysis has helped brands identify untapped opportunities in multicultural segments, resulting in average revenue growth of 40% for her clients.

Angie holds a Bachelor's degree in Marketing from Northwestern University and is certified in Retail Management from the National Retail Federation.
